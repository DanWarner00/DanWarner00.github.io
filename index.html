About

Analyst, builder, finisher.

I'm a data analytics programmer from Portland with a BS in Business Analytics from the University of Oregon. Most of my professional time has been inside a federal energy efficiency program, building the Python tooling, MongoDB pipelines, and compliance reports that helped identify 40,000 eligible households across 30+ Ohio counties for weatherization funding under the Inflation Reduction Act.

What ties my work together is building automations that save people time and empower the employees who use them. At Candle I'm co-building an agentic AI platform that takes the repeatable office work a team would otherwise grind through and hands it to AI agents, which do the job and check in for approval when it matters. On DinoQuote I wired up the Stripe billing, CRM sync, and SendGrid email automation that lets small HVAC contractors run their back office without a full ops team. Same instinct every time: find the manual, repetitive thing and let software carry it.

So here I am with over two years of real program experience, a handful of side projects I actually finished, and a willingness to point my skills at whatever needs solving. Whether it's energy, AI, or operations, if there's messy data or a manual workflow begging to be automated, I'm interested. I also spend a lot of my own time keeping up with the newest AI tooling, so the stack I reach for is rarely more than a few weeks behind the cutting edge.

Outside of work: cooking, lifting, playing basketball, golf, discovering new board games, and being a Blazers fan, which builds character if nothing else.
